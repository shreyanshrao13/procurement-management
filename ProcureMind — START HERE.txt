# ProcureMind – Quick Start Guide (Non-Technical Users)

### Step 1: Open the Application

Locate the file named **ProcureMind.html** and double-click it.

It will automatically open in your web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari).

No installation, coding, or setup is required.

---

### Step 2: Choose How You Want to Use It

You have two options:

**Option A – Try the Demo**

* Click **"Load Demo Data"**
* The application will load sample products and cost information automatically.
* This is the fastest way to see how ProcureMind works.

**Option B – Use Your Own Data**

* Open the **"00 – My Data"** section.
* Upload your CSV files containing:

  * Product/BOM data
  * Material prices
  * Labor costs
  * Supplier quotes
  * Historical purchase orders

The system will calculate should-costs using your data.

---

### Step 3: Review the Should-Cost Analysis

After loading data, ProcureMind will automatically show:

* Estimated should-cost
* Material cost breakdown
* Labor cost breakdown
* Overhead costs
* Supplier margin estimate
* Key cost drivers

This helps you understand what the product should realistically cost.

---

### Step 4: Compare Supplier Quotes

Upload or enter supplier quotes.

ProcureMind will compare:

* Supplier quoted price
* Estimated should-cost
* Historical purchase prices
* Market benchmarks

It will highlight:

* Overpriced suppliers
* Fair pricing range
* Potential savings opportunities

---

### Step 5: Generate a Negotiation Brief

Click **"Negotiation Brief"**.

The system will generate a one-page summary containing:

* Recommended target price
* Walk-away price
* Negotiation points
* Cost breakdown
* Savings opportunities

You can print or share this report before supplier meetings.

---

### Important Notes

✅ No software installation required

✅ Works completely offline

✅ Your data stays on your computer

✅ No internet required for CSV uploads

✅ Simply open **ProcureMind.html** and start using it
